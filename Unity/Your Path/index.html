About: Your Path

Your Path is the first (and as of writing only) game I have made in Unity.
Therefore, making this project has been a great learning experience for me: developing my familiarity with Unity, C# (the programming language it uses for scripting), procedural generation, and game making in general. Needless to say, I really enjoyed said experience, and will certainly be making similar games in the future!

This game was inspired by Kurzgesagt's video on Optimistic Nihilism, a philosophy that has inspired and shaped me, and which I try my best to live by.
In the game I wanted to make, however, I wanted to be more specific, and focus on the idea that we should aim to create our own goals, direction, and future that we wish to pursue (an idea I am determined to stick by as I advance through education); for only then can we find meaning in our own lives.
And so I came up with gameplay teaching the player to create their own path and explore the forest around them - in their own direction at their own pace - and not to blindly follow the path laid out for them.

In the end I am pleased with the game I created; sure, it may be unoptimised, and the models are most certainly not stylish or realistic, but that's not what I set out to achieve.
