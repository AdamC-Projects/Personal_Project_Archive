About: Neural Net

This way a project where I tried to create a functioning neural network from scratch in python. Because its been so long since I've used some of my earlier projects, I can't remember quite how they work...
I only upload them here really so you can appreciate, and I can remember, the full journey I went through iterating on my neural network designs before my final project ( ＾v＾)
