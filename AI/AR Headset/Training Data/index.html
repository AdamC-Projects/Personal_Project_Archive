Data Creator

In order of the AI to be able to learn how to detect hands, I need to provide it with annotated training data. In most AI projects, this is a mammoth task, however the smaller scale of my project made it a bit of a smaller task, and so I took around 450 photos (which were each cropped to become 200x150 images). To make my life easier, I wrote code to automatically do all of this, so all it required me to do was to wear the headset and move my hands to different positions on the display.

Semi-automatic Annotator

Now that the images have been taken, I needed to annotate them.

The images are split into 48 25x25 images, and if I think a hand is in one, I label it 1, else 0.
The 48 images are then saved.

In order to automate this process, I created a python script that lets me click on the sections of the image where I think a hand is to label them 1, and then when I press enter, its splits the image into the 48 25x25 images, with the filename including the label (1 or 0).
