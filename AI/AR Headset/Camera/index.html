Camera


Because I wanted to make an AR headset, I needed two displays on screen at once of the camera, side by side. I could not find a way to achieve this with the picamera module for python, so instead I used mmalobj, the layer below picamera.

I messsed around with the display sizes and positions and I was surprised that my brain was able to interpret the displays when I had the headset on, no matter the size or the offset... it was quite an eye opener, you could say... :--)
It just goes to show how dynamic our brains are, designed with no specification as to what body type they will be controlling ( ＾v＾)
